Nurturing Future Leaders

8 Years of Excellence

Welcome to Samata Pathshala Kailali

Located in Lamkichuha-1, Lamki, Kailali, Nepal, Samata Pathshala is dedicated to providing quality education under the leadership of Principal Lokraj Devkota.

Our motto "Small Step, Big Future" reflects our belief that every small step in education leads to a brighter future for our students and community.

Our Programs

Academic Excellence

Primary Education (Classes 1-5)

Our primary program focuses on foundational literacy and numeracy skills while nurturing curiosity and creativity through interactive learning methods.

  • Child-centered learning approach
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Creative arts integration
  • Character development programs
Learn More

Lower Secondary (Classes 6-8)

Our lower secondary program builds on foundational skills while introducing more specialized subjects with project-based learning approaches.

  • Subject specialization
  • Science and technology focus
  • Leadership development
  • Extracurricular activities
Learn More

Secondary Level (Classes 9-10)

Our secondary program prepares students for SEE examinations with advanced curriculum and career counseling for future academic challenges.

  • Exam-focused curriculum
  • Career counseling
  • Advanced science labs
  • Research skills development

Our Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Modern Library

Our well-stocked library provides students with access to thousands of books, digital resources, and a quiet study environment to foster a love for reading and research.

  • 10,000+ books collection
  • Digital reading stations
  • Reading clubs and activities

Science Laboratories

Fully-equipped science labs allow students to conduct experiments and engage in hands-on learning to complement theoretical knowledge.

  • Modern chemistry lab
  • Advanced biology lab
  • Well-equipped physics lab

Sports Complex

Our sports facilities include a football field, basketball court, and indoor games to promote physical fitness and teamwork among students.

  • Football field
  • Basketball court
  • Indoor games facilities
